Welcome to the Underground...

Hip-hop originated in the predominantly African American, economically depressed South Bronx section of New York City in the late 1970s.

Elements of Hip Hop Music & Culture Include:

  1. Deejaying aka "Turntabling"
  2. Rapping aka "MCing" or "Rhyming"
  3. Graffiti Painting aka "Tagging"
  4. B-Boying Encompasses Hip-Hop Dance, Style & Attitude
