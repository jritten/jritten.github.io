Hip Hop Makes the World Go 'Round

The Hip Hop Cause is vital to the preservation and growth of the Hip Hop community. The Hip Hop Caucus supports the underserved Hip Hop communities by educating, engaging and mobilizing these communities in civic, social and political processes. To learn more about how you can contribute to these underserved communities visit the Hip Hop Caucus website.
